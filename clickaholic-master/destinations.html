Bandekal gudda :


BandeKallu Gudda provides a beautiful hiking trails around the Mallandur area, in close proximity to Muthodi. “Bande Kallu Gudda” is an interesting hiking place from Chikmagalur. Surrounded by coffee plantations, the road will turn into a steep gully-eroded jeep track and gains significant elevation within a km of distance.
